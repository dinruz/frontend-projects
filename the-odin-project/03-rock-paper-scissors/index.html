ROCK-PAPER-SCISSORS

🕹️ NEW GAME 🕹️

RULES

🟡 It's you vs. Machine!

This is a simple three-choice game.
You will play against the PC until one of you wins the match.

🟡 The Logic

In each round, choose one of three hand shapes.
Each shape can beat only one other shape :

  • Rock beats Scissors(1 pt.)
  • Scissors beats Paper(1 pt.)
  • Paper beats Rock(1 pt.)
  • Matching moves - tie (0 pts.)

🟡 The Winner

The first player to reach 5 points wins the game. Good Luck!🍀
